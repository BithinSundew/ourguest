How to Reach

By Air

Gangtok is 150 kms approximately away from the nearest airport i.e. Bagdogra Airport(IXB). Flights are available from all major cities.It takes one couple of hours to travel from Bagdogra to Gangtok. On the way you can experience the beauty of the himalayan roads, rivers, hills, tea gardens and Green Valleys.

By Train

The new Jalpaiguri Railway Station is the closest broad gauge railway station from Gangtok that connects to all other major cities in India.
