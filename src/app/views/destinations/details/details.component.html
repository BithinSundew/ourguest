Little Singamari Homestay

Darjeeling, West Bengal, India

Share

Situated just 2 km away from the town, the Homestay overlooks the Singtam valley with picturesque tea gardens. Many tourist places like Zoo, HMI, Ropeway, Tenzing Rock etc are within walking distance. We offer spacious clean rooms and home cooked food.

The homestay offers a meditation room and a Buddhist stupa within the campus. Factory visit at Happy valley Tea Estate, short treks, jungle walk, logistic for local site seeing can be organized on request. A spectacular view of Mount Kanchenjunga, the third highest mountain in the world, towering at a height of 28169 feet/8586 meters is just a mere 5 mins walk from our home.

St Josephs School and College established over 125 years ago are in the same vicinity. For guests spiritually inclined there is a church nearby, where there is daily mass offering in the morning. Leisurely morning walks with the cool air and mountain view can be a thing to cherish for a long time after leaving the Queen of the hills, Darjeeling.

How to Reach

By Air

Darjeeling is 100 kms approximately away from the nearest airport i.e. Bagdogra Airport(IXB). Flights are available from all major cities.It takes one couple of hours to travel from Bagdogra to Darjeeling. On the way you can experience the beauty of the himalayan roads, rivers, hills, tea gardens and Green Valleys.

By Train

To reach Darjeeling by train, one may opt to reserve the ticket on the Darjeeling Himalayan narrow gauge that connects Darjeeling from New Jalpaiguri. It is one of the most prestigious as well as heritage journey in India. Well, the new Jalpaiguri Railway Station is the closest broad gauge railway station from Darjeeling that connects to all other major cities in India.

The toy train for Darjeeling starts at the New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) and goes all the way to Darjeeling travelling a distance of 88kms. There are of course short joy rides and other short distance connections. In most part the railway track runs parallel with the Hill Cart Road and at many points even crisscrosses it.The diesel loco hauled train takes about 7 hours 15 minutes to reach its destination.
