I want to make changes in my booking done on OurGuest.in, how do I do it?

Write to us at contact@ourguest.in or call our support number (+91 9734813101, +91 7031272572) between 9 am to 8 pm on all days. We will make changes accordingly if it is possible.

What are the prices for different group size (3, 5,6, and higher)?

Please select the tour package of your choice and mention the number of guests travelling in the send enquiry form. We will get back to you with the prices accordingly. In every tour package mentioned on OurGuest’s website, the prices reduce as the number of people increases.

Does OurGuest help in obtaining travel permits in restricted areas?

Yes, we help in getting travel permits for our guests.

Does OurGuest help in booking flight tickets too for the travel?

No. We are really sorry but currently we do not provide flight or train booking facilities

What all kinds of stay options does OurGuest provide?

OurGuest mainly provides homestays, resorts, retreats and farmstays as our accommodation option. We believe our guests will enjoy their holidays better in these places vis-a-vis commercial hotels. We provide standard and premium stay arrangements depending upon your choice of package. If the guest wants to stay in a commercial hotel, we can likewise provide commercial hotel accommodation too.

Does OurGuest provide weekend getaways of short duration ranging from 2-3 days?

Yes, OurGuest provides short weekend getaways as well as long tours. All our tours on the site are customizable and we can tailor it according to your requirements. Please check the tour package you like and mention your requirements in the send enquiry form. We will accordingly get in touch with you to help you design the tour package that suits you.

Does OurGuest help in providing information about the best places to explore depending upon the season?

Yes, OurGuest provides information about the best destinations in Sikkim and North East India. Please write to us in our Contact page and our team will get in touch with to help you choose the best places.

What is the cancellation policy of OurGuest?

For tour package cancellations, please check the cancellation policy in the tour page. If cancellations are made 30 days or more prior to scheduled trip start date, you will get full refund minus Rs 500 service fee. For Homestay cancellations, we provide full refund minus service charge (Rs 500) if the cancellation is made before 30 days of scheduled arrival. Within 30 days of scheduled arrival, the cancellation policy will be as per the respective property cancellation policies. We will provide you these details via email before you book with us.

How do I know my booking is confirmed?

After you make an initial payment, an automated message will come to your registered email id with the confirmation.

What is the advance amount that I have to pay for booking tour/homestay with OurGuest?

50% advance payment needs to be made to book the tour package. For homestay booking, guests need to pay 20% advance if they are booking 30 days prior to scheduled arrival. Within 30 days of scheduled stay, guests need to pay full amount to book the accommodation.
