Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2020

You don’t want to worry about the whereabouts of your private information when you’re searching for travel inspiration or planning your next adventure. That’s why we’re committed to protecting the personal information you share with us – and why we’ve created this notice. It tells you who we are, the ways we collect your information, what we do with it, and who we share it with.

Who we are

OurGuest Global, Inc. is based in Wyoming, USA and is the parent company for the global OurGuest group. When we refer to “OurGuest”, “we”, “our”, or “us” in this notice, that means OurGuest Global, Inc. on behalf of the OurGuest group.

OurGuest Global, Limited in the Republic of Ireland is our representative for any data protection issues within the European Economic Area. You can raise any issue with your local supervisory authority wherever you are in the EU, or otherwise the regulator where you are.

OurGuest is a leading travel media company and the world’s number one travel guidebook brand. OurGuest owns and operates www.ourguest.com Trips by OurGuest, and Guides by OurGuest.

Your information – how we collect it, what we collect, and why
We collect information about you when:
  • you give it to us or one of the companies in our group (for example, when you sign up to receive our emails or create a profile on our website or mobile apps),
  • you post content in our products and services (for example, posts you might make to Thorn Tree or within our mobile application Trips by OurGuest) or via our social media accounts, and
  • sometimes automatically (for example, technical information that transfers when you view our sites or applications on your phone or computer).

We explain each of these in more detail below, but to be clear, OurGuest does not, and will not, sell information about you to anyone. We only use your information as set out in this notice and we will only share your information with third parties for commercial purposes such as marketing or promotions if we have your consent to do so; or where we’ve engaged a company outside OurGuest to provide a service for us (which might involve them handling your information, but this is only when we have proper safeguards in place); or where we’re obliged by law to share information with authorities.
